# Product

## Register

product

## Users

35mm serves film lovers, cinephiles, filmmakers, and film professionals who use
the product in fast-moving social contexts: reading a feed, discussing cinema,
sharing work, managing relationships, and tracking activity. Interfaces should
help people understand and act without requiring knowledge of internal systems.

## Product Purpose

35mm is the social layer around cinema: a place where people log what they
watch, share what they make and think, discover films and filmmakers, and build
community. Success means social actions feel immediate, trustworthy, and
human while deeper film context remains easy to reach.

## Brand Personality

Conversation-first, cinematic, and thoughtful. Voice is direct and warm without
being cute, corporate, legalistic, or overly technical. Trust-and-safety moments
should feel calm, clear, respectful, and personal.

## Anti-references

- Developer consoles, database viewers, audit logs, and internal admin tools on
  public user surfaces.
- Generic enterprise settings dashboards with repeated labels, nested cards,
  dense metadata, or implementation vocabulary.
- Loud punitive safety language, opaque legal copy, or UI that makes reporters
  feel accused, ignored, or exposed.
- Decorative styling that competes with posts, people, films, or conversation.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with the user's question, not the system's data model.
2. Show familiar social objects as people originally experienced them.
3. Explain outcomes plainly while protecting everyone’s privacy.
4. Keep primary actions and status understandable at a glance.
5. Preserve continuity with the feed, profiles, notifications, and settings.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Support keyboard and screen-reader navigation, visible focus,
semantic status announcements, sufficient contrast in every theme, reduced
motion preferences, zoom and narrow mobile layouts, and meaning that never
depends on color alone. Safety copy must remain clear for non-native speakers
and people under stress.
